Acceleration is invariant under GR:

$$
a = \frac{d^{2}x}{dt^{2}}=\frac{d^{2}x'(t)}{dt'^{2}}=\frac{d^{2}}{dt'^{2}}(x-vt)=\frac{d^{2}x}{dt^{2}}-\underset{ =0 }{ v\frac{d^{2}t}{dt^{2}} }=a
$$
